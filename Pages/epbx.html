What is an EPBX System?

  • An Electronic Private Branch Exchange (EPBX) manages internal and external communication within your organization.
  • It routes calls between users on local lines while saving costs on external calls.
  • Provides features like voicemail, call forwarding, conference calling, and more, to enhance productivity.
  • Integrates with other communication tools like email, video conferencing, and CRM systems for seamless collaboration.

What Do Our EPBX Services Include?

  • Consultation and Design: We analyze your communication needs and design a custom EPBX system tailored to your organization’s size and requirements.
  • Installation: Professional setup of the EPBX hardware, including phones, servers, and network equipment.
  • Configuration: Setting up the system to include features like call routing, voicemail, extensions, and more for your team.
  • Integration: We connect the EPBX system to your existing communication tools, like email or video conferencing platforms.
  • Maintenance and Support: Ongoing service to ensure smooth operation, updates, and troubleshooting when needed.

Why Choose an EPBX System for Your Organization?

  • Cost Savings: Reduces the cost of external communication by routing calls over internal lines.
  • Scalability: Easily add new extensions or features as your business grows.
  • Enhanced Communication: Includes features like call forwarding, voicemail, and conference calling to support efficient internal and external communication.
  • Reliability: Ensures uninterrupted communication for teams and clients.
  • Advanced Features: Integrates with CRM and other tools to enhance team collaboration and customer service.
